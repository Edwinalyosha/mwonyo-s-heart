About Mwonyo's Heart Foundation

Mwonyo's heart is a youth based Non government organization geared towards helping those in need.

ABOUT US

Mwonyo's Heart foundation was started in 2021 by students and guild cabinet members of Uganda Christian University Mukono with a goal of helping those in the community around them that were in need. The activities of the organization were all centered around the student body at the start until the organization was finally registered two years later and the outreach opened up to the entire country.

OUR VISION:

A society in which those in need can realise their full potential in life. Where one has enough to eat to stay alive, is educated and healthy enough to earn a living and thrive in life

OUR MISSION:

In order to enable those in need to earn a livelihood, alleviate poverty, or eliminate it entirely, we aim to help those in need by putting up jobs for them to earn a living. In order to house the homeless and end hunger, we aim to give enough food and shelter to those in need. In order to help the less fortunate people maintain their health and well-being, we aim to build health centres that can offer medical services for free.

OUR GOALS:

  • To eradicate poverty in our communities
  • Achieve Zero Hunger.
  • Promote Good Health and Well-Being
  • Provide quality education; key to poverty eradication.
  • Achieve Gender Equality
  • Provide clean water and sanitation.
